Tabineko

By Toshinori Mori

Our exhibition

Tabineko is a series of illustrations which literally means "cat goes on a trip". The series features a black cat who travels with his friends through various places, like city streets or county lanes.

Toshinori is also fascinated by the seasons, which are constantly changing Japanese landscape - you can notice this on each illustration.
